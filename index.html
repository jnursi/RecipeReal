Banana Pancakes

Ingredient Amount
Bananas 2
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Cinnamon 1 tsp
  1. Mash bananas
  2. Mix in the egg, vanilla and cinnamon
  3. Pour pancake batter onto a griddle
  4. Cook until bubbles pop on the surface of the pancake
  5. Flip the pancake and cook the other side
  6. Enjoy
